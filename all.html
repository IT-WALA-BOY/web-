HONESTY IS THE BEST POLICY

Honesty is the best policy for the reason that without honesty, people wouldn't be able to rely on you. The aspect that builds trustworthy and reliable relationships has everything to do with honesty. ... If you're honest with your friends and certain relationships, you're proving to them that you can be trusted.Honesty implies being truthful. Honesty means to develop a practice of speaking truth throughout life. A person who practices Honesty in his/her life, possess strong moral character. An Honest person shows good behavior, always follows rules and regulations, maintain discipline, speak the truth, and is punctual.
